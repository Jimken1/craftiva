Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to use?

Yes! Basic use of Craftiva, including the gallery and referrals, is completely free. Some advanced features like the apprentice directory and leaderboard require a subscription.

How do I earn money?

You can earn through our generous referral program, by taking on paid jobs as an apprentice, and by participating in surveys and competitions (coming soon!).

Can non-creatives use the platform?

Absolutely! You can sign up as a member to browse the gallery, discover amazing talent, and hire skilled apprentices for your projects.

Can I pay in my local currency?

Yes! Our payment system includes a currency calculator to show you the equivalent price in your local currency before you pay.
